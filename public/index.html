Easebuzz Payment Gateway

Test payment using your sandbox credentials.

How to test successfully:
1. Keep UPI + Net Banking selected
2. On Easebuzz page, choose UPI or Net Banking
3. For UPI sandbox: use test UPI ID shown on the page
4. For Net Banking: pick any test bank and complete the flow
Card error GC0C05?
Your merchant account does not support international cards. The test Visa/Mastercard numbers from Easebuzz may be blocked. Use UPI or Net Banking instead, or ask Easebuzz support to enable domestic/international cards on your account.
